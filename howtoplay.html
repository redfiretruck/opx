Basic Training

When you start a new game, you go to the control panel. This is where you live now. There's text there, tabs and memos. Read it all. It'll keep our assets alive.

Your job is to help and guide the assets. These are people on the ground loyal to us, highly trained and fully equipped people. Read the briefing, it'll explain the mission objectives. Look at the intelligence photos, maps and everything else. Do it quick, the asset will need help. In SpyGraph time has a way of passing pretty quickly.

You know about the implants yet? Our guys and girls behind enemy lines have Subvocal Recognition Implants. These let them chat in real time with us, without making it obvious they are communicating. The chat window needs your constant attention. The subdermal interface isn't 100% accurate, and most of the assetes aren't native English speakers, so stick to short simple commands.

. what do you see
. where are you
. go to the watchtower
. find the detonator
. eliminate target
. sitrep
. standby

You need to know the assets are working in tough conditions, they are ultimately in charge and won't walk off a cliff just because you tell them to.

Follow the brief, do what you're told and our guys will get out in one piece. Don't mess it up, lives are on the line. We aren't authorized to threaten you directly, but we're all adults here so do as your told and don't be squeamish.
